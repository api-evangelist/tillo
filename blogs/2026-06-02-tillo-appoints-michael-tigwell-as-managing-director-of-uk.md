Tillo has named Michael Tigwell as Managing Director for the UK and EMEA regions as the rewards and gift cards company enters an expansion phase. Tigwell brings over 15 years of SaaS scaling experience, having previously served as Chief Customer Officer and Chief Operating Officer at Benefex, where he helped grow the organization from 100 to 1,300 employees. His appointment positions Tillo to accelerate growth across its core markets while CEO Alex Preece focuses on expanding into the United States.
