Tillo Partners with Core Rewards to Modernize Enterprise Incentive Programs Tillo, the global gift card and rewards platform, has partnered with Core Reward s, a provider of enterprise incentive and recognition programs, to expand enterprise rewards programs with travel, dining, entertainment and lifestyle eGift card offerings. Through Tillo’s global gift card API , Core Rewards can now offer participants flexible digital rewards including flights, hotels, cruises, restaurants and activities through a seamless redemption experience.
