Tillo announced a partnership with Flight Centre Canada, integrating the travel brand's gift cards into its gift card, rewards, and incentives API platform so Canadian businesses can offer travel rewards through employee recognition, consumer incentives, cashback platforms, loyalty schemes, and digital gift card marketplaces. The announcement cites Flight Centre Canada research that 82% of Canadians view vacations as essential to health and wellbeing.
