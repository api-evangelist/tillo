<h2>Joyogram and Tillo Partner to Globalize Digital Rewards and Team Celebrations</h2> 
<p>Joyogram, the premier digital celebration platform, has integrated Tillo’s global gift card network to enhance how distributed teams celebrate milestones. This partnership allows businesses to pair personalized group cards with flexible digital rewards from over 3,000 brands worldwide.</p>
